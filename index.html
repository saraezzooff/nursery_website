Welcome!

Kinder Preschool was established in 1998 by Peter McMillan with the main goal of providing primary education through a stimulating curriculum with help from a very devoted and nurturing staff.

Our goals are accomplished through the efforts of a professional, caring, and dedicated staff that inspire children, involve parents and support colleagues, so that all children successfully achieve a love of learning, confidence in themselves, and respect for others.We always try to make our preschool an optimum environment for all children.

Our Centre

We have been in operation since 1998, for children from 2 to 6 years old.

We believe it is our responsibility to provide environments and experiences that capture the joys of childhood, nurture each child’s individual growth and development.

Activities

Our well-rounded curriculum based on free play, group and individual activities.

The various activities included in the curriculum are based on varied monthly themes such as space, fire, water, earth, myself, family, people around us, birds, animals etc.
